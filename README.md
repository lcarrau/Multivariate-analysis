# Multivariate-analysis
Final project for the Master's course: "Multivariate analysis"

PISA is a triennial survey of 15-year-old students worldwide to assess their acquisition of key knowledge and skills essential for full participation in social and economic life. PISA assessments not only determine whether students nearing the end of their compulsory education can reproduce what they have learned, but also examine their ability to extrapolate and apply their knowledge in unfamiliar contexts, both inside and outside of school.

This questionnaire asks questions about students' experiences with economic issues both inside and outside of school.
